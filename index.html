About me

I am a UX/UI Designer with experience in digital product design and graphic design.

I’ve graduated from the University of Buenos Aires as a Fashion Designer but I found my true passion in the world of UX and UI Design. I admire how thoughtful design has the power to change our lives and I have a great interest in designing and investigating aspects that could improve user experiences.

I am highly goal-oriented, I am always looking for improvement and I am well adaptive to a constantly changing environment.

I have experience designing apps, websites and webapps. I can solve problems, create functional experiences and digital interfaces focus on user needs.
