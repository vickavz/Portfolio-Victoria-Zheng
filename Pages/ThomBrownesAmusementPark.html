Thom Browne's Amusement Park

"In 1950, break free from routine and plunge into our whimsical and fantastical realm. Welcome to Thom Browne's amusement park."

We have crafted a collection comprising an experimental, ready-to-wear, and casual series, drawing inspiration from the universe of designer Thom Browne. Through a surreal and ironic lens, Browne taps into American stereotypes and customs of the high social class from previous decades. He utilizes tailoring as a tool to satirize the clichés associated with his social class. The designer consistently reevaluates the past (retro) by intervening and redefining traditional typologies, incorporating new technologies and morphological changes. The primary innovation lies in applying tailor techniques to various types of clothing. We embarked on a journey to the 1950s, immersing ourselves in the universe of American amusement parks to create our distinctive collection.
