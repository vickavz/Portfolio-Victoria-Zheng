Fashion Talents App Design

​​​​​​​"Fashion Talents" is a meeting point between professionals and fashion companies from all over the world. It´s an app where designers can exhibit their projects and experiences, which reach a wide audience including recognized companies and fashion brands.

In this case study, I designed a mobile application specialized in the self-promotion of fashion designers, where each user can reveal their work experiences, exhibit their designs and connect with other professionals in this field, and on the other hand, companies can find new talents and provide job opportunities.
