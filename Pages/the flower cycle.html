The Flower Cycle

A short series of textiles has been designed to portray the compelling story of the flower cycle. Drawing inspiration from the works of Japanese designer Akira Minagawa for his brand Mina Perhonen, particularly the captivating "Letter of Flower." This design seamlessly blends Japanese and Scandinavian influences to artistically depict stylized scenes of nature and everyday life. Minagawa's creative process commences with hand-drawn motifs, employing various techniques to craft tactile and intricately detailed textile patterns.
