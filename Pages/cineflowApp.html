Cineflow App Design

As part of my Google UX Design Professional course, my first project started out by choosing a topic using the tool Sharpen to generate a random design challenge. I was given the task of "Design a mobile ticketing app for a movie theater." Going to the cinema is a fun experience, but I found out that ordering tickets in many apps and websites often feels like navigation is a really long and frustrating process. So, in this case study I came up with the idea of designing an easy-to-use cinema ticketing app for a hypothetical movie theater "CineFlow".
